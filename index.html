Belt Conveyor System - Formulas & Concepts

1. Belt Tension (T)

Formula: T = Te + Ts + Tr

Where:
Te = Effective Tension
Ts = Slack Side Tension
Tr = Resistance Tension

2. Power Required (P)

Formula: P = (T × V) / 1000

Where:
T = Tension (N)
V = Belt speed (m/s)

3. Belt Speed

Formula: V = π × D × N / 60

Where:
D = Pulley Diameter (m)
N = Rotation speed (RPM)
